A White Man's World

Hollywood's dire need for more representation of BIPOC (Black, Indigenous, and People of Color) and female directors is crucial for fostering diversity within the film industry. Their voices bring fresh narratives, cultural insights, and authentic storytelling, enriching the cinematic impact and resonating with diverse audiences worldwide. Increasing their visibility not only enhances the creative diversity of films but also serves as a catalyst for positive social change, promoting greater understanding and unity among audiences.

That being said, here's yet another insuffiecient list of BIPOC/Female Directors

  • Joaquim Dos Santos
  • Peter Ramsey
